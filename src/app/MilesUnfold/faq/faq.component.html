Frequently Asked Questions

About the Startup

We are Miles Unfold, a startup committed to enriching the educational and fun experiences of school students through meticulously designed trips and excursions. We believe in extending learning beyond the classroom and into the real world.
We believe that real-world experiences can significantly enhance students' understanding of various subjects. Our philosophy is to make learning immersive, engaging, and enjoyable.
Our team of experienced educators and trip planners carefully designs each itinerary to ensure that it is both educational and fun. We collaborate closely with schools, teachers, and parents to make sure our trips meet and exceed their expectations.

Our Services

We provide uniquely curated educational trips and excursions. Our itineraries include visits to places of historical, scientific, cultural, or natural interest, with guided activities to help students learn and grow.
Our trips are designed with learning objectives in mind. We identify key educational themes for each location and plan activities that align with these themes. This could involve learning about history at a museum, understanding ecosystems on a nature hike, or exploring art at a gallery.
We cater to school students of all ages. Our trips are designed to be age-appropriate, ensuring that students of different levels get the most out of their experience.

Booking and Logistics

You can book a trip with us by visiting our website. Choose the desired trip and follow the instructions for booking. For any assistance, feel free to contact our support team.
The cost of our trips includes all transportation, accommodation, meals, entrance fees for visits, and guides for the duration of the trip. For specifics, please refer to the itinerary details of the chosen trip.
Safety is our top priority. We ensure that all our excursions are supervised by experienced guides. Our team is trained to handle emergencies, and we always have a detailed emergency response plan in place. We also work closely with schools and parents to ensure any specific safety needs are met.
You can contact us via the form on our or reach out to us through email or phone. We'll be glad to answer any further questions you may have.
